About the Theme

“The World According to Derrick” is a handcrafted WordPress theme inspired by the timeless look of classic newspapers. Featuring a bold masthead, elegant typography, and a structured multi‑column layout, it brings a refined editorial feel to modern storytelling. Designed and developed by Derrick D. Patton of SiteCraft Studio.
