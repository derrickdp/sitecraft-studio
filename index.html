What We Do

I have decided to take a step forward and put my more than 25 years of IT skills to work by building websites for small businesses. I am both ready and excited for this next chapter in my career.
I believe every business, no matter its size, deserves a professional online presence that reflects its unique identity and helps it grow. My goal is to provide affordable, functional, and visually appealing websites that can support small business owners in reaching their customers more effectively.

Enterprise-level expertise, sized for your business

I spent two decades building systems for some of the biggest names in finance and media. I’m proud of that work, but I got tired of pouring my skills into companies that already had armies of engineers. I wanted to bring that same level of reliability and professionalism to small businesses, nonprofits, and community organizations that usually get stuck with cookie-cutter solutions. That's why I started SiteCraft Studio, so owners like you can have enterprise-level technology without paying enterprise prices.
