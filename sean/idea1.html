About Us

Built on Kentucky Roots & Honest Work

We're a family-owned general contracting business right here in Monticello, KY. We've been building and renovating homes across Wayne, McCreary, and Pulaski counties for over 15 years — and we treat every project like it's our own home.

When you hire us, you're not getting a franchise or a crew from out of state. You're getting neighbors who know this land, understand what Kentucky homes need, and stand behind every nail we drive.
